# SSC Inventory System — Order Flow

## Overview

The SSC Inventory system manages the full lifecycle of a sales order — from creation by the sales team through fulfilment, billing, and final delivery. There are four teams involved: **Sales/Ops**, **Fulfilment Centre (FC)**, and **Accounts/Billing**.

## Order Lifecycle

### Stage 1 — Order Creation (Ops)

- Ops team receives a customer purchase order
- Creates a new order in the system with:
  - Customer name, GSTIN, PO number
  - Order items (item codes, quantities, prices)
  - Dispatch address, credit terms, fulfilment centre
- Order is assigned a unique **SO / CO number** (e.g. SO-2026-0001)

---

### Stage 2 — Dispatch Planning (Ops)

- Ops reviews the order and creates a **Dispatch Batch**
- A temporary DC number is assigned automatically (e.g. `Temp/DC0001/26-27`)
- Items are allocated to the batch with quantities and prices
- Order moves to FC for fulfilment

---

### Stage 3 — Fulfilment Centre (FC)

The FC team works through three steps:

#### 3a. Picking
- FC picks all items for the batch from warehouse storage
- Marks order as **Picked**

#### 3b. Packing
- FC packs all picked items ready for dispatch
- Marks order as **Packed**

#### 3c. Goods Issue
- FC issues goods from the warehouse
- The temporary DC number is **confirmed** to a permanent DC (e.g. `SSC/DC0001/26-27`)
- Order is handed over to Accounts for billing
- A **Delivery Challan PDF** can be downloaded at the Delivery Ready stage (after invoice and transport details are available)

---

### Stage 4 — Billing / Accounts

The Accounts team processes the order through four steps:

#### 4a. Credit Check
- Accounts reviews the customer's payment status
- **Two options:**
  - ✅ **No Pending Payment — Go Ahead** → Order proceeds to GI Posted
  - ⚠️ **Pending Payment — Take Approval** → `Credit Override` flag is set, order stays for approval. A red "Take Approval" badge appears on the order.

#### 4b. Goods Issue Posted
- Accounts posts the Goods Issue entry in SAP / accounts system
- A temporary invoice number is assigned automatically (e.g. `Temp/INV0001/26-27`)
- Order moves to Invoice Generation

#### 4c. Invoice Generation
- Accounts confirms the final invoice
- Temporary invoice becomes permanent (e.g. `SSC/INV0001/26-27`)
- **Invoice PDF upload is mandatory** at this step (max 100 KB)
- Order moves back to FC for delivery arrangement

#### 4d. E-Way Bill
- After FC sets delivery details, Accounts generates the E-Way Bill
- E-Way Bill number is entered manually
- **E-Way Bill PDF upload is optional** (max 100 KB)
- Order moves to FC for final delivery confirmation

---

### Stage 5 — Delivery (FC)

- FC receives the order back after E-Way Bill is generated
- Delivery details are already set (vehicle number, driver, transport mode)
- FC confirms the order has been **delivered to the customer**
- If all items are delivered → Order is marked **Complete**
- If partial delivery → Order returns to Ops for next batch

## Complete Status Flow

```
Order Created (Ops)
       ↓
Dispatch Batch Created (Ops)  →  Temp DC assigned
       ↓
FC: Picking
       ↓
FC: Packing
       ↓
FC: Goods Issue  →  DC confirmed (Temp → Permanent)
       ↓
Accounts: Credit Check
       ↓                    ↓
  Go Ahead              Take Approval (flag set, awaits approval)
       ↓
Accounts: GI Posted  →  Temp Invoice assigned
       ↓
Accounts: Invoice Generated  →  Invoice confirmed + PDF uploaded
       ↓
FC: Set Delivery Details  →  Vehicle, driver, transport mode
       ↓
Accounts: E-Way Bill  →  E-Way number + optional PDF
       ↓
FC: Confirm Delivered
       ↓
Order Complete ✓
```

## Key Documents Generated

| Document | Stage | Required |
|----------|-------|----------|
| Delivery Challan (DC) | After Delivery Ready | Auto-generated PDF |
| Invoice | Invoice Generation | Mandatory upload (max 100 KB) |
| E-Way Bill | E-Way Bill stage | Optional upload (max 100 KB) |

---

## DC Challan Contents

The Delivery Challan PDF is auto-generated by the system and includes:

- SSC Control Pvt. Ltd. letterhead and logo
- Customer name, address, GSTIN
- Challan number and date
- SO / Order number reference
- PO number and date
- Invoice number reference
- Vehicle number (if applicable)
- Items table with quantities, unit prices, net values
- CGST (9%) + SGST (9%) breakdown
- Grand total and amount in words
- Signature blocks (Prepared By / Checked By / Authorised Signatory)
